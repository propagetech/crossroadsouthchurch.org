Welcome to Crossroad South Church!

We are a community of Christ-followers from diverse linguistic, geographic, and cultural backgrounds.

The deepest desire of this community is to bring glory to the Triune God and to enjoy their relationship with Him. All are welcome to this church. We eagerly look forward to meeting and sharing a meaningful relationship with you.

Corporate worship and small groups are conducted in English. We are kids, youth, and adult friendly.

Our Mission

The mission of Crossroad South Church is to Exalt the Triune God, Edify fellow believers and Evangelize the unreached.

Core Values

Our core values are: breaking down barriers, Gospel-centered, deeply biblical, missional living.

Our Vision

To be a platform for individual and families, living in South Bangalore to meet with Jesus Christ and to preach-teach the Word to them so as to make Christian discipleship a lifestyle.
