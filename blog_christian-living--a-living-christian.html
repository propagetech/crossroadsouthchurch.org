Christian Living, A Living Christian

Scripture Passage: Rom 12:1-2
Recap & Introduction
•“The Just shall live by Faith”: The Word of God that is True over the centuries
•God’s Glory, His Presence and His characteristics of Mercy and Justice
•To be Justified, To Live and To be Faithful
•We who have been Justified and in whom God has authored Faith are called to Live a Faithful Life
Christian Living – Rom 12:1
•I urge you
•Therefore, in view of God’s mercy
•God calls us to do something only after providing for the calling
•Offer your Bodies
•As a Living Sacrifice, Holy and Pleasing to God
•Your true and proper Worship
Christian Living – Rom 12:2
•Do not conform to the pattern of this world (Amos 7:7, 8)
•Be transformed by the renewing of your mind (Isa 55:8, 9)
•Test and Approve God’s will (Psalm 34:8)
A Living Christian - called to Serve
•Think with the Right Perspective
•God has dealt each one a measure of Faith to be Faithful *
•Many Members and One Body
A Living Christian - called to Love
•No Love, No Gain (1 Cor 13:1 – 3)
•Love the feeling is the Fruit of Love the Action *
•To Serve is to Love and to Love is to Serve *
Take Away:
I urge you…
To Serve the Lord Jesus Christ by Knowing Him and Living a Faithful Life that is characterized only by Christian behavior!
