OUR WORSHIP SERVICE

Corporate Worship

The Corporate Worship service is from 10:30 am to 12 noon, each Sunday. The worship involves a time of intercession, praise and preaching of the Word.

Kingdom Warriors

Children in two age groups 3-7 and 8-13 years meet for Sunday School in parallel with the main worship time during the sermon time. The Sunday School curriculum is built in-house and follows a parallel track with what is preached in the main worship.

Teens Ministry

Teens meet every alternate Sunday in parallel with the preadhing time. The time of gathering revolves around a study of the Word, and its application in daily discipleship.

Womens Ministry

Women meet every alternate Sunday right after service. A time of active learning from the Word and praying.
